MP4 Video Delivered from CDN

The video https://imsstorage.blob.core.usgovcloudapi.net/videofilespub/samplevideo.mp4 has not been transcoded.
The video is sent using the Azure Content Delivery Network (CDN).

  • Transcoding and viewing frameworks are not necessary to view video on desktop browsers.
  • Backward and forward play.
  • CDN sends the video using servers that are closest to the user.
